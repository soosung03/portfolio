About Me

Soosung Yang

Hello! I'm Soosung Yang, a passionate front-end web developer and an avid photographer who finds inspiration both in code and in the cities. My love for photography started with a long trip to Korea, and it evolved into a lifelong passion. Capturing the bauty of the night cities fuels my creativity, whether I'm desiging websites or planning my next photography shoot.

As a Computer Science student at University of Oregon, I specialize in creating responsive and engaging digital experiences. From crafting sleek portfolio websites to developing interactive web applications, I enjoy merging technology with creativity. My skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript allow me to build intuitive interfaces that enhance user engagement.

My goal is to inspire others to embrace both technology and the cities. Whether you're looking for web development insights or recommendations on night time walking experience, I’d love to connect! Let's create, explore, and make every step—both online and city adventure worth taking.
